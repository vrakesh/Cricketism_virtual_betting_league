GAMEPLAY RULES:

  1. For each game one can bet 10,20,30 or 50 points for either side
  2. Every player can bid 50 on a team Five times for the entire tournament
  3. If you bet on the loosing side, you loose points equivalent to the points you bet
  4. If you bet on the winning side, you gain (betting_points) * (loosing_side_odds/winning_side_odds)

SAVE TIPS AND SUGGESTIONS

  1. When you change the points, it instantly saves
  2. There are two elements in the UI, a drop box and label below that
  3. The dropbox always defaults to "home-side"-"10" and the label is the actual saved value
